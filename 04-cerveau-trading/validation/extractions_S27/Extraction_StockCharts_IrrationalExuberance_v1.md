### D2343 — Mécanisme d'amplification 1 : changement d'attitude (« stocks for the long run »)
🔵 **ÉCOLE** (Robert Shiller) (Source : irrational_exuberance.md) : « amplification mechanisms intensified the effects. First, there was a change in investor attitudes toward stocks. By the late 1990s stocks were considered a long-term investment that could not go wrong... The S&P 500 then went on a 10-year stretch of underperformance. In fact, the S&P 500 was trading below its 2000 level in early 2011. This means 11 years of negative returns for buy-and-hold investors that bought in 2000. »
**TRADEX-AI C5** : Mécanisme d'amplification — la croyance « les actions ne peuvent que monter » précède une décennie perdue. Leçon : l'excès de confiance collectif marque souvent les sommets.

🔵 **ÉCOLE** (Robert Shiller / StockCharts) (Source : irrational_exuberance.md) : « Just as prices trend, valuations also trend from overvaluation to undervaluation.
