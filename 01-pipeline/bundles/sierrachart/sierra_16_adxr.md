### Description

This study calculates and displays the Welles Wilder's Average Directional Movement Index Rating (ADXR).

The ADXR is based on calculations similar to those used in the ADX . Just as in that study, the DX Length and DX Mov Avg Length Inputs are denoted as \(n_{DX}\) and \(n_{ADX}\), respectively.
