Trading at Marker (TAM) is analogous to Trading at
Settlement (TAS) wherein parties are permitted to trade
at a differential to a not-yet-known price. TAM uses a
marker price, whereas TAS uses the Exchange-
determined daily settlement price for the underlying
futures contract month.
