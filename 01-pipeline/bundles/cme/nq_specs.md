BTIC trading terminates at 4∶00 p.m. ET on the Thursday

requirements of Rule 524.D. TMAC trades off a "Base
Price" of zero to create a differential versus the daily
TAM OR TAS RULES Marker at Close price on the underlying futures contract
month. The TMAC clearing price equals the daily Market
at Close price of the underlying futures contract month
plus or minus the TMAC transaction price.
