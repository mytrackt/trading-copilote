# Economic Indicators

## Overview <a href="#overview" id="overview"></a>

StockCharts provides data for a select group of economic indicators. Depending on the data series, these are updated on a weekly, monthly or quarterly basis. For example, chartists can plot weekly Initial Jobless Claims ($$UNEMPCIN) or monthly Non-farm Payrolls ($$EMPLOY). These can then be compared to the S\&P 500 for analysis.

## Details <a href="#details" id="details"></a>

**Symbol Group:** Economic Indicators

**Publisher:** Federal Reserve Economic Data (FRED)

**Update Frequency:** Weekly, Monthly, or Quarterly

**Online Source:** St Louis Fed Economic Research

**Links to Current Symbols:** [Economic Indicators](https://stockcharts.com/search/?q=%22Economic%20Indicators%22\&section=symbols)
