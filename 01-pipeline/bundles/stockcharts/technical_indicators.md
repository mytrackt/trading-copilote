# Technical Indicators

**Technical Indicators** are the often squiggly lines found above, below, and on top of the price information on a technical chart. Indicators that use the same scale as prices are typically plotted on top of the price bars and are therefore referred to as **“Overlays.”**

{% hint style="warning" %}
**Cool Tip.** If you are new to stock charting and the use of technical indicators, the following article will help you get started: [**Introduction to Technical Indicators and Oscillators**](/table-of-contents/technical-indicators-and-overlays/introduction-to-technical-indicators-and-oscillators.md). It's an in-depth introduction to various technical indicators and oscillators—a “must-read” article for StockCharts users.

## Technical Indicators <a href="#technical_indicators" id="technical_indicators"></a>

[**Accumulation/Distribution Line**](/table-of-contents/technical-indicators-and-overlays/technical-indicators/accumulation-distribution-line.md) \
Combines price and volume to show how money flows into or out of a stock.

[**Aroon**](/table-of-contents/technical-indicators-and-overlays/technical-indicators/aroon.md)\
Determines if a stock is trending or consolidating using the Aroon Up and Aroon Down.

[**Aroon Oscillator**](/table-of-contents/technical-indicators-and-overlays/technical-indicators/aroon-oscillator.md)\
Measures the difference between Aroon Up and Aroon Down.

[**ATR Bands**](/table-of-contents/technical-indicators-and-overlays/technical-indicators/atr-bands.md)\
A volatility-based indicator that plots bands above and below a moving average of the underlying price.

[**ATR Trailing Stops**](/table-of-contents/technical-indicators-and-overlays/technical-indicators/atr-trailing-stops.md)\
A volatility-based indicator that sets dynamic stop-loss levels for market exits or entries.&#x20;

[**Average Directional Index (ADX)**](/table-of-contents/technical-indicators-and-overlays/technical-indicators/average-directional-index-adx.md)\
Shows whether a stock is trending or oscillating.

[**Average True Range (ATR)**](/table-of-contents/technical-indicators-and-overlays/technical-indicators/average-true-range-atr.md)\
Measures a stock's volatility.

[**Average True Range Percent (ATRP)**](/table-of-contents/technical-indicators-and-overlays/technical-indicators/average-true-range-percent-atrp.md)\
Measures a stock's volatility, expressed as a percentage for easier cross-symbol comparison.

[**Balance of Power (BOP)**](/table-of-contents/technical-indicators-and-overlays/technical-indicators/balance-of-power-bop.md)\
Measures buying and selling pressure to determine which side is in greater control and driving price action.

[**Bollinger BandWidth**](/table-of-contents/technical-indicators-and-overlays/technical-indicators/bollinger-bandwidth.md)\
Shows the percentage difference between the upper and lower Bollinger Band.

[**%B Indicator**](/table-of-contents/technical-indicators-and-overlays/technical-indicators/b-indicator.md)\
Shows the relationship between price and standard deviation Bollinger Bands.

[**Chaikin Money Flow (CMF)**](/table-of-contents/technical-indicators-and-overlays/technical-indicators/chaikin-money-flow-cmf.md)\
Combines price and volume to show how money may be flowing into or out of a stock Alternative to Accumulation/Distribution Line.

[**Chaikin Oscillator**](/table-of-contents/technical-indicators-and-overlays/technical-indicators/chaikin-oscillator.md)\
Combines price and volume to show how money may be flowing into or out of a stock. Based on Accumulation/Distribution Line.

[**Chande Trend Meter (CTM)**](/table-of-contents/technical-indicators-and-overlays/technical-indicators/chande-trend-meter-ctm.md)\
Scores the strength of a stock's trend, based on several technical indicators over six different timeframes.

[**CMB Composite Index**](/table-of-contents/technical-indicators-and-overlays/technical-indicators/cmb-composite-index.md)\
An unbound momentum oscillator that attempts to improve on some of the shortcomings of traditional RSI.

[**Commodity Channel Index (CCI)**](/table-of-contents/technical-indicators-and-overlays/technical-indicators/commodity-channel-index-cci.md)\
Shows a stock's variation from its “typical” price.

[**ConnorsRSI**](/table-of-contents/technical-indicators-and-overlays/technical-indicators/connorsrsi.md)\
A momentum oscillator created from the composite of RSI, ROC, and Up/Down Length, designed for shorter-term trades.

[**Coppock Curve**](/table-of-contents/technical-indicators-and-overlays/technical-indicators/coppock-curve.md)\
An oscillator that uses rate-of-change and a weighted moving average to measure momentum.

[**Correlation Coefficient**](/table-of-contents/technical-indicators-and-overlays/technical-indicators/correlation-coefficient.md) \
Shows the degree of correlation between two securities over a given timeframe.

[**DecisionPoint Price Momentum Oscillator (PMO)**](/table-of-contents/technical-indicators-and-overlays/technical-indicators/decisionpoint-price-momentum-oscillator-pmo.md) \
An advanced momentum indicator that tracks a stock's rate of change.

[**Detrended Price Oscillator (DPO)**](/table-of-contents/technical-indicators-and-overlays/technical-indicators/detrended-price-oscillator-dpo.md) \
A price oscillator that uses a displaced moving average to identify cycles.

[**Distance From Highs**](/table-of-contents/technical-indicators-and-overlays/technical-indicators/distance-from-highs.md) \
Shows how far a security is trading from its 3, 6, 9, or 12-month highs, expressed as a percentage.

[**Distance From Lows**](/table-of-contents/technical-indicators-and-overlays/technical-indicators/distance-from-lows.md) \
Shows how far a security is trading from its 3, 6, 9, or 12-month lows, expressed as a percentage.

[**Distance To Highs**](/table-of-contents/technical-indicators-and-overlays/technical-indicators/distance-to-highs.md)\
Shows the percentage move necessary for a security to reach a 3, 6, 9, or 12-month high.

[**Distance To Lows**](/table-of-contents/technical-indicators-and-overlays/technical-indicators/distance-to-lows.md) \
Shows the percentage move necessary for a security to reach a 3, 6, 9, or 12-month low.

[**Distance From Moving Average**](/table-of-contents/technical-indicators-and-overlays/technical-indicators/distance-from-moving-average.md) \
Shows the percentage difference between price and a moving average of your choice.

[**Ease of Movement (EMV)**](/table-of-contents/technical-indicators-and-overlays/technical-indicators/ease-of-movement-emv.md) \
An indicator that compares volume and price to identify significant moves.

[**Force Index**](/table-of-contents/technical-indicators-and-overlays/technical-indicators/force-index.md) \
A simple price-and-volume oscillator.

[**Gopalakrishnan Range Index**](/table-of-contents/technical-indicators-and-overlays/technical-indicators/gopalakrishnan-range-index.md)\
Determines the range of price moves for different assets and identifies which investments are more volatile and which tend to move in slow and consistent trends.

[**High Low Bands**](/table-of-contents/technical-indicators-and-overlays/technical-indicators/high-low-bands.md)\
Identifies and analyzes price trend direction, overbought/oversold conditions, support and resistance levels, and volatility.

[**High Minus Low**](/table-of-contents/technical-indicators-and-overlays/technical-indicators/high-minus-low.md)\
Measures the difference between a financial asset's highest and lowest prices per day or session.

[**Linear Regression R2**](/table-of-contents/technical-indicators-and-overlays/technical-indicators/linear-regression-r2.md)\
An oscillator that indicates the presence of a strong trend or a lack of trend.

[**Mass Index**](/table-of-contents/technical-indicators-and-overlays/technical-indicators/mass-index.md) \
An indicator that identifies reversals when the price range widens.

[**MACD (Moving Average Convergence/Divergence Oscillator)**](/table-of-contents/technical-indicators-and-overlays/technical-indicators/macd-moving-average-convergence-divergence-oscillator.md) \
A momentum oscillator based on the difference between two EMAs.

[**MACD-Histogram**](/table-of-contents/technical-indicators-and-overlays/technical-indicators/macd-histogram.md) \
A momentum oscillator that shows the difference between MACD and its signal line.

[**MACD-V**](/table-of-contents/technical-indicators-and-overlays/technical-indicators/macd-v.md) \
A volatility-normalized MACD that can analyze trends.

[**MACD-V Histogram**](/table-of-contents/technical-indicators-and-overlays/technical-indicators/macd-v-histogram.md) \
A momentum oscillator that indicates if a trend is positive or negative in absolute terms.

[**Median Price**](/table-of-contents/technical-indicators-and-overlays/technical-indicators/median-price.md)\
Displays a trading range's midpoint for a given period.

[**Money Flow Index (MFI)**](/table-of-contents/technical-indicators-and-overlays/technical-indicators/money-flow-index-mfi.md) \
A volume-weighted version of RSI that shows shifts is buying and selling pressure.

[**Negative Volume Index (NVI)**](/table-of-contents/technical-indicators-and-overlays/technical-indicators/negative-volume-index-nvi.md) \
A cumulative volume-based indicator used to identify trend reversals.

[**On Balance Volume (OBV)**](/table-of-contents/technical-indicators-and-overlays/technical-indicators/on-balance-volume-obv.md) \
Combines price and volume in a very simple way to show how money may be flowing into or out of a stock.

[**Percentage Price Oscillator (PPO)**](/table-of-contents/technical-indicators-and-overlays/technical-indicators/percentage-price-oscillator-ppo.md) \
A percentage-based version of the MACD indicator.

[**Percentage Volume Oscillator (PVO)**](/table-of-contents/technical-indicators-and-overlays/technical-indicators/percentage-volume-oscillator-pvo.md) \
The PPO indicator applied to volume instead of price.

[**Performance Spread**](/table-of-contents/technical-indicators-and-overlays/technical-indicators/performance-spread.md)\
A relative strength indicator that considers the difference in percentage changes between a security and a baseline symbol.

[**Price Relative / Relative Strength**](/table-of-contents/technical-indicators-and-overlays/technical-indicators/price-relative-relative-strength.md)\
Technical indicator that compares the performance of two stocks to each other by dividing their price data.

[**Pring's Know Sure Thing (KST)**](/table-of-contents/technical-indicators-and-overlays/technical-indicators/prings-know-sure-thing-kst.md)\
A momentum oscillator from Martin Pring based on the smoothed rate-of-change for four different timeframes.

[**Pring's Special K**](/table-of-contents/technical-indicators-and-overlays/technical-indicators/prings-special-k.md)\
A momentum indicator from Martin Pring that combines short-term, intermediate and long-term velocity.

[**Rate of Change (ROC) and Momentum**](/table-of-contents/technical-indicators-and-overlays/technical-indicators/rate-of-change-roc.md) \
Shows the speed at which a stock's price is changing.

[**Relative Strength Index (RSI)**](/table-of-contents/technical-indicators-and-overlays/technical-indicators/relative-strength-index-rsi.md) \
Shows how strongly a stock is moving in its current direction.

[**Relative Volume (RVOL)**](/table-of-contents/technical-indicators-and-overlays/technical-indicators/relative-volume-rvol.md) \
A set of indicators/overlays that compare current volume to the average, in order to identify conditions where unusually high or low volume is powering a price move.

[**RRG Relative Strength**](/table-of-contents/technical-indicators-and-overlays/technical-indicators/rrg-relative-strength.md) \
Uses RS-Ratio to measure relative performance and RS-Momentum to measure the momentum of relative performance.

[**StockCharts Technical Rank (SCTR)**](/table-of-contents/technical-indicators-and-overlays/technical-indicators/stockcharts-technical-rank-sctr.md)\
Our relative ranking system based on a stock's technical strength.

[**Slope**](/table-of-contents/technical-indicators-and-overlays/technical-indicators/slope.md)\
Measures the rise-over-run for a linear regression.

[**Standard Deviation (Volatility)**](/table-of-contents/technical-indicators-and-overlays/technical-indicators/standard-deviation-volatility.md)\
A statistical measure of a stock's volatility.

[**Stochastic Oscillator (Fast, Slow, and Full)**](/table-of-contents/technical-indicators-and-overlays/technical-indicators/stochastic-oscillator-fast-slow-and-full.md) \
Shows how a stock's price is doing relative to past movements. Fast, Slow and Full Stochastics are explained.

[**StochRSI**](/table-of-contents/technical-indicators-and-overlays/technical-indicators/stochrsi.md) \
Combines Stochastics with the RSI indicator to help you see RSI changes more clearly.

[**Traffic Light**](/table-of-contents/technical-indicators-and-overlays/technical-indicators/traffic-light.md)\
Shows bullish (green), bearish (red), and neutral (yellow) zones on a chart using parameters you set.

[**TRIX**](/table-of-contents/technical-indicators-and-overlays/technical-indicators/trix.md)\
A triple-smoothed moving average of price movements.

[**True Strength Index**](/table-of-contents/technical-indicators-and-overlays/technical-indicators/true-strength-index.md) \
An indicator that measures trend direction and identifies overbought/oversold levels.

[**TTM Squeeze**](/table-of-contents/technical-indicators-and-overlays/technical-indicators/ttm-squeeze.md)\
A volatility indicator that identifies periods of consolidation and determines the direction of the resulting move.

[**Typical Price**](/table-of-contents/technical-indicators-and-overlays/technical-indicators/typical-price.md)\
An indicator that represents a trading bar's average price.

[**Ulcer Index**](/table-of-contents/technical-indicators-and-overlays/technical-indicators/ulcer-index.md)\
An indicator designed to measure market risk or volatility.

[**Ultimate Oscillator**](/table-of-contents/technical-indicators-and-overlays/technical-indicators/ultimate-oscillator.md)\
Combines long-term, mid-term, and short-term moving averages into one number.

[**Vortex Indicator**](/table-of-contents/technical-indicators-and-overlays/technical-indicators/vortex-indicator.md)\
An indicator that identifies the start of a new trend and defines the current trend.

[**Weighted Close**](/table-of-contents/technical-indicators-and-overlays/technical-indicators/weighted-close.md)\
An indicator that identifies a prevailing trend.

[**Williams %R**](/table-of-contents/technical-indicators-and-overlays/technical-indicators/williams-r.md)\
Uses Stochastics to determine overbought and oversold levels.
